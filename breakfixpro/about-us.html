About BreakFixPro

Revolutionizing vehicle breakdown assistance in Nepal

Our Story

BreakFixPro was founded in 2025 by a team of automotive engineers and tech entrepreneurs who recognized a critical gap in Nepal's vehicle support infrastructure.

After experiencing firsthand the challenges of finding reliable mechanics during vehicle breakdowns, especially in remote areas, our founders set out to create a solution that combines AI technology with local expertise.

Today, BreakFixPro has grown into Nepal's leading platform for vehicle breakdown assistance, connecting thousands of drivers with verified mechanics while providing AI-powered diagnostics that empower drivers to make informed decisions about their vehicles.

Our Mission

At BreakFixPro, we're dedicated to revolutionizing vehicle breakdown assistance in Nepal through cutting-edge AI technology and a comprehensive network of reliable mechanics.

Our Vision

To create a world where vehicle breakdowns are no longer stressful events, but manageable situations with immediate, reliable solutions at your fingertips.
