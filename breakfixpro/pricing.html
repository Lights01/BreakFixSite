Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be applied at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a contract or commitment?

No, all our plans are month-to-month with no long-term contract required. You can cancel anytime.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, eSewa, Khalti, and bank transfers for all paid plans.

Are there any additional fees?

No hidden fees. The price you see is the price you pay, inclusive of all taxes and service charges.

What if I'm not satisfied with the service?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for all new Premium and Pro subscriptions if you're not completely satisfied.
